Your Vote Matters

Your Vote Matters will be a voter information website providing you everything you need to know to be a successful and independent thinking voter. The website will provide information such as, Political Party platforms, affiliation quizzes, where and how you can vote, and who and what is on the ballot.

With the upcoming 2022 Midterm Election and the 2024 Election, Your Vote Matters will also be utilized to fundraise and spread the word about the Democratic Parties achievements.
